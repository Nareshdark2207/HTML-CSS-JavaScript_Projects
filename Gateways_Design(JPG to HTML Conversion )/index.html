About

Institutional access to overlooked real estate opportunities
Gateway provides institutional access to opportunities that are overlooked by larger players in the real estate industry.

In the past twenty yearsthe Gateway principals have invested in, developed or co-developed +2,000 apartment units or approximately 2.5 million square feet of mixed-use, mixed-income, and student housing assets across 13 markets in 7 states. Additionally,they have led and participated in over $15 billion of real estate debt & equity transactions with leading institutions and sponsors.

The Gateway team has established a strong reputation for sourcing off-market deals and converting opportunities that others failed to recognize across a wide array of transactions (oa. development, acquisition, debt restructuring).

Gateway applies an institutional mindset to unlock value in off-center opportunities. The firm partners with capable groups (developers, landowners) who lack the ability to capture full potential on their own. Creativity and experience allow the Gateway team to create complimentary partnerships and tailor capital markets solutions to deliver compelling returns in even the most challenging of circumstances.

MUNICIPALITIES

As demand for urban living continues to evolve, abandoned property or underutilized city assets can be key components of a completely new vision for revitalizing a community. Gateway ties in government and community interests to build news-worthy projects.

DEVELOPERS

Developers can immediately benefit from Gateway’s global network and access to capital. Local Developers create unparalleled access to projects. With Gateway as a partner, long planned visionary projects can make it to fruition.

CHURCHES + NON-PROFITS

Non-Profit Institutions and Churches are the center of most communities. Many times they will accumulate, through acquisitions or bequeath, assets that require investment, real estate expertise or a lengthy development process. Gateway can provide a clear path to true growth and community benefit.

UNIVERSITIES

With the national university system expanding rapidly, maximizing student housing, university facilities and underutilized owned assets are now key factors to an institution’s long term success. Gateway has the experience to bring out the full value and pride in an institution’s assets.
